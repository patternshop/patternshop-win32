Choose a background colour
All you need to do is click on the rectangular zone of the background tracing pattern to open the colour selector, which can be configured in RGB, Hue, Saturation or Luminosity mode:


Attention : To export the network to a transparent background, you need to deactivate the display of the background in project window, clicking on the "eye" icon to one side:



You can then import a template, going to stage 6, and save or export your document, going to stage 7.
